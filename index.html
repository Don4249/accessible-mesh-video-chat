2-4 Person Video Chat!

No downloads required - works right in your browser!
Tested with Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.

1. Copy and paste the Custom Room Link into your browser.
2. Click "Allow Camera/Mic access while visiting the site" if prompted.
3. Click "START/JOIN" button to start or join a call already in progress.
If you are first person starting the call, just wait for others to join.
If you are the 2-3-4 person to join, you will automatically enter.
4. You are now ready to chat with others.

Scroll down for more controls after joining.
Note: You can have at most 2-4 people in the call!

📖 Video Chat Guide

🚀 Quick Start

1. Click "START/JOIN" and allow camera/microphone access

2. Share your room link with family (shown above)

3. Wait for others to join or join their call

💡 Tip: Bookmark your room link for easy access next time!

🎮 Control Buttons (Bottom Bar)

  • ❓ Help - Full User Guide - Complete Video Chat Guide / Help?
  • 🎤 Mute - Turns your microphone on/off (red = muted)
  • 📞 Leave - Exits the call and returns to start screen
  • 📷 Camera - Turns your camera on/off (red = off)
  • 💻 Share - Shares your screen (present documents, photos, etc.)
  • 📸 Snap All - Take photo (png) of each participant
  • 💻 Prt Scr - Capture entire screen, all videos plus control buttons, saves as png image
  • 🚪 Close - Ends the call and closes the app completely

🖥️ Controls on Each Person's Video

  • 🖥️ Full Screen - Makes that video full screen (press ESC to exit)
  • 📤 Send Zip - Sends a ZIP file to that person
  • 📥 Accept Zip - Accepts an incoming file (pulses green when receiving)

💻 Share

  • Click '💻 Share' button on the
  • computer control bar to share files with other users. Most
  • often photos will be shared but other types of files
  • such as docs, text files, etc. Mobile devices are not
  • allowed to share their files but can view files shared
  • from a computer.
  • A dialog box will appear. I always select the 'Entire
  • Screen' option, giving you more control over the sharing
  • process. Then click on the picture of the full screen
  • and then click on the 'Share' button.
  • Use the file browser to select the location of the files
  • to share and then click on the first file to share.
  • Enlarge to full screen if needed. You may also roll the
  • mouse wheel to zoom on a portion of the photo. You can
  • click and drag the mouse to scroll to a different part
  • of the enlarged view. Click the small triangles on the
  • left or right side of the photo to move to the next or
  • previous photo. When all done showing images, click
  • 'Stop Sharing' button. Now navigate bck to the video chat.

📁 File Transfer

  • Only ZIP files are supported (great for sharing multiple photos)
  • Click "Send Zip" on the person's video to choose a file
  • Click "Accept Zip" when someone sends you a file
  • A progress bar shows transfer status
  • Files save to your computer's Downloads folder
📌 Note: Chrome/Edge users get a save dialog. Firefox/Safari users get a download prompt.

💻 Prt Scr

  • Click 'Prt Scr' button on the
  • control bar to save a photo of the full screen.
  • When on a computer, a dialog box will appear. Please
  • select the location to save the full screenshot.
  • You also have the option to change the name of the file.
  • Click the 'Save' button.
  • When on a mobile device, the screenshot will be saved
  • automatically to your device's default photo gallery
  • or downloads folder.

💻 Snap All

  • Click '📸 Snap All' button on the
  • control bar to save a photo of each user's full screen.
  • If 3 or 4 users are in the room, you will be asked if
  • you want to download multiple files. Click 'Allow'.
  • When on a computer, a dialog box will appear. Please
  • select the location to save the first remote user's video.
  • You also have the option to change the name of the file.
  • Click the 'Save' button. Same for next user.
  • When on a mobile device, the dialog box also asks for
  • download permission for each user. Each snapshot will be
  • saved automatically to your device's default photo gallery
  • or downloads folder.

🔑 Creating Your Own Private Room

Add ?room=YourRoomName to the end of the URL

Example: https://meshvideochat.fly.dev/?room=smith-family

Bookmark your custom room link and use it every time!

💡 Tip: Use letters, numbers, and hyphens only (example: texas-family-2024)

🔧 Troubleshooting

  • No video? - Click the camera button to turn it on
  • No sound? - Click the mic button to unmute
  • Can't connect? - Refresh the page and try again
  • Screen share not working? - Make sure you allow screen capture permission
  • Others can't see you? - Make sure you clicked "Allow" for camera/mic
